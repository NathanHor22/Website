Who we are
Syntech Solutions
is a tech-driven innovator redefining vocational training through immersive technologies. Our flagship project, SynthSnip, combines VR, AI, and custom-built haptic gloves to deliver realistic, sustainable, and cost-effective hairdressing and barbering education, transforming how hands-on skills are taught and experienced.
What we do
Next Gen VR Barbering
Using Unity XR and custom-built haptic gloves, we offer a realistic, cost-effective, and sustainable alternative to traditional mannequin-based training. Our technology reduces material waste while delivering hands-on, tactile feedback that simulates real haircutting, helping students learn faster, smarter, and greener.
